## Root Cause Analysis

The 12.5-point gap is NOT because CandB is a bad model. It's because the **live ensemble pipeline degrades healthy round predictions**:

1. **NN weight curve caps healthy at 20%** — at z=0.454, the NN only contributes 20% of the blend. The other 80% is Dirichlet prior, which is less accurate on healthy rounds.

2. **Dirichlet prior is too confident on Land cells** — puts too much mass on "stays empty" and not enough on settlement/ruin alternatives.

3. **The nightforce forward score (90.35) is raw model output** — no Dirichlet dilution. This proves the NN alone is better than the NN+Dirichlet blend on healthy rounds.
